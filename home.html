New To Townsville? Love Music? Become a member today!

Are you really passionate music player? Do you really enjoy listening to a large variety of music styles? Or do you simply enjoy the atmosphere of live performances as well as discounts on tickets for these events? You could then benefit from the awesome offer of becoming a member of the Townsville Community Music Centre. You can get awesome discounts for your favourite bands, promote your favourite artists by adding their profile to the website as well as many other benefits! Simply head to the membership page to fill out your form, either online or post it to us and for only $25 a year you can immideately start enjoying the benefits of becoming a member today!

If you have any questions feel free to email admin@townsvillemusic.org.au
